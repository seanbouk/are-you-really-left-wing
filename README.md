# Are You Really Left Wing?

A short quiz that measures where you fall on 12 independent political scales — from economics to governance to personal freedom.

The point: there's no single "left" or "right." Most people are closer to the centre than they think, and you can disagree with someone on one thing while agreeing on plenty of others.
