Are You Really Left Wing?

Or right wing, for that matter?

We tend to think of politics as a single line from left to right. But "left" and "right" mean very different things depending on what you're talking about.

This quiz measures where you fall on 12 independent scales — from economics to governance to personal freedom. You might be surprised how much you agree with people you thought you disagreed with.

There are no right or wrong answers. Pick whichever feels most true to you.
